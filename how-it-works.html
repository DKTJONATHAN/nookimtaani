How Nooki Mtaani Works

Whether you have space to rent or need to find space, we make it simple

1

List Your Space in Minutes

Create a free listing with photos, detailed description, and set your own pricing. Our simple form guides you through the process.

  • No upfront costs
  • Set your availability
  • Add multiple photos
2

Receive Booking Requests

Get notified when renters are interested in your space. Communicate through our secure messaging system.

  • Real-time notifications
  • Screening tools for renters
  • Calendar to manage bookings
3

Get Paid Securely

We handle all payments via M-Pesa. You receive 90% of the rental fee (we take just 10% as commission).

  • Instant M-Pesa payments
  • Payment protection
  • Monthly earnings reports
1

Search for Spaces

Use our filters to find the perfect space by location, price, size, and amenities. Save your favorites for later.

  • Map view for locations
  • Detailed space descriptions
  • Real photos of each space
2

Book Your Space

Contact the owner through our platform, ask questions, and book instantly for available dates.

  • Secure messaging
  • Instant booking options
  • Calendar availability
3

Pay & Use the Space

Pay securely via M-Pesa and access the space during your booked time. Our platform protects both parties.

  • Secure M-Pesa payments
  • Booking confirmation
  • Owner contact details
